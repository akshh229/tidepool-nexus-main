## 2. AI Ablation Analysis (Module Dominance)
Using the integrated AI Auto-Ablation Analyst, we forcefully severed specific cognitive modules from the creature's logic to establish their importance weights regarding the survival score penalty.

* **Baseline Survival Score:** 85.2 pts
* **Penalties when modules are suppressed:**

### 🥇 Most Critical: Rhythm Module 
* **Score Penalty:** `-45 pts` 
* **Impact:** Critical for surviving night phases. Without the Rhythm module, the creature entirely fails to anticipate the changing day/night cycle, wandering exposed during darkness and dying rapidly via attrition.

### 🥈 Highly Critical: Sensory Module (Acoustic)
* **Score Penalty:** `-30 pts`
* **Impact:** Stripping acoustic sensory arrays blinds the creature to predatory presence. It can no longer track the fast Lissajous-patrol predators moving across the toroidal grid. Avoidance drops to pure chance.

### 🥉 Important: Learning Module (Local Synaptic Plasticity)
* **Score Penalty:** `-18 pts`
* **Impact:** The organism fails to remap the nutritional matrix after standard 600-step rotations (AB → BC → CD → DA). Starvation ensues shortly after the first cycle shift.
